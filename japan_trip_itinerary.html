📝 Important Notes

Transportation

• OMO5 Otsuka — Near Otsuka Station (JR Yamanote Line)

• Narita Express — Direct to Shinjuku Station

• Takanawa/Oimachi Area — Keihin-Tohoku Line or taxi from Shinjuku

• Karuizawa — Rental car or taxi recommended for scattered attractions

Reservations

• Tabelog (https://tabelog.com) — Japan's main restaurant review site

• Many restaurants accept reservations via Tabelog or phone

• Popular restaurants may require reservations, especially dinner

• Walk-in places like Torikizoku and Ichiran don't need reservations

Weather (July 2026)

• Tokyo July: Hot and humid (30-35°C)

• Bring water, sunscreen, and light clothing

• Many restaurants have air conditioning

• Consider indoor activities during hottest part of afternoon

Budget Estimate (per person)

• Day 2: Lunch ¥1,000 + Dinner ¥3,000-5,000 = ¥4,000-6,000

• Day 3: Lunch ¥1,500 + VR ¥2,000 + Dinner ¥3,000-5,000 = ¥6,500-8,500

• Day 4: Lunch ¥1,000 + Dinner ¥3,000-5,000 = ¥4,000-6,000
